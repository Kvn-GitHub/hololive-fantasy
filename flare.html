Flare

Flare is a kind and extremely humble individual, almost to a fault. Her kindness is vouched for most often by the other hololive Fantasy members, which has resulted in her so-called 'harem,' with Pekora being an exception. However, out of the four members in hololive Fantasy, it remains to be Noel who is the most blatant in her affection.

Timeline
  • 2020 - On 14 November, she debuted her third 2D costume during a stream and elegant gray and black dress with a black sweater, she also wears a choker with a golden ornament, Flare also has the option to wear glasses, her mascot Kintsuba also received a matching outfit now wearing a suit with a hat and a golden mask and a cane.
